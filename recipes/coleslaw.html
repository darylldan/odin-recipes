🥗 Coleslaw Salad

Goes great with barbecues and picnics. For a more mild salad, use less green onions. Good for 8 servings.

By Allrecipes Member from allrecipes.com


🛒 Recipe:

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 medium head green cabbage, rinsed and very thinly sliced
  • 1 large carrot, shredded
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 radishes, sliced

📜 Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, lime juice, cumin, salt and pepper.

  2. Add the cabbage, carrot, green onions and radishes and stir until well-combined.

  3. Chill at least an hour before serving.

🍴 Enjoy your meal!
